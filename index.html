iEARN (International Education And Resources Network) is the largest non-profit global network that enables teachers and youth to use the Internet and technologies to collaborate on projects that enhance learning and make a difference in the world. iEARN is working in more than 150 countries all over the world , connecting more than 2,000,000 students , teachers and Alumnus from all over the world.
